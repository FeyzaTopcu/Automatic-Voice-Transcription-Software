"Of the tree fruits, the apple is known to have been widely cultivated from an early date. 

The wild varieties were also much in demand by early peoples, carbonized apples from about 6500BC have been found at Catal Huyuk in Anatolia. 

The Swiss prehistoric lake-dwellings revealed not only large quantities of sour crabs which seem to have been a very important food at the time, but also larger ones which appear to have been of a cultivated variety. 

These were mainly cut into two pieces, which suggests that they were dried, while the smaller ones were left whole...
